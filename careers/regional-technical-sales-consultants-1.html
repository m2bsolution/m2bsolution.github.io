Regional Technical Consultants

13.10.21
Australia, UK/Europe, North America
Full time

Datum360 is a market leader in the delivery of SaaS based software for Engineering Information and Asset Management systems to the global AEC sectors.

The Datum360 Connected Data Platform is recognised as one of the most comprehensive, open, configurable, trusted data backbones for the management of engineering information, a trusted place to go to get to the truth about engineered assets quickly, securely, and efficiently. We provide our Connected Data Platform to the process, energy, mining, utilities & infrastructure sectors.

As a trusted data backbone, the Connected Data Platform is a critical component (for the whole lifecycle of an asset) that is necessary to build collaboration environments involving, for instance, engineering design systems, asset management, BIM, field data collection, 3D visualisation, GIS, and workflow.

Our Connected Data Platform is foundational to the emerging world of Digital Twins as each Digital Twin needs a trusted data backbone to be able to even get off the ground.

As the foundational component for multiple business functions, the Connected Data Platform is open and works on the principle of easy in / easy out, providing easy integration using a range of intelligent REST API and messaging techniques.

Due to the significant ongoing success of the business, we are looking to recruit a number of Technical Consultants with experience in relevant sectors and who have had involvement in asset information and software or data integration projects. These consultants will help evangelise, demonstrate, and articulate the Datum360 Connected Data Platform value to engineering service providers, contractors, owner operators and equipment vendors. Industry sector and sales environment experience, preferably from an operations or engineering background, are important.

We are looking for people who would relish making a difference within a team-oriented environment.

About the role

As a Technical Consultant, reporting to the Managing Director, you will be involved in every stage of the client engagement, supporting sales staff in a pre-sales capacity, preparing technical solutions and delivery plans that achieve the client's desired outcomes. As a key member of the global team, you will be called on for your experience and opinion to assist in the growth of the business, alongside delivery of individual customer successes.

You will focus most of your time on the evangelising, design, and delivery of the Connected Data Platform, which includes:

  • Working alongside our fast-moving sales team providing industry knowledge and support to our clients
  • Engaging with clients at all levels in their organisation and building on current relationships
  • Assisting clients to mitigate risk and improve their efficiency

About you

You will be an experienced Solution Engineer/Consultant who will have a set of skills, characteristics, and experience across a range of the key requirements below:

  • Be passionate about delivering the best customer experience and solutions
  • Have demonstrable experience in any of the process, energy, mining, utilities, and infrastructure sectors
  • Show a good understanding of the data topic alongside a sound insight into Information Management challenges in these sectors.
  • Experience of software development, particularly integration patterns and use of REST APIs
  • A background in operations or engineering, with a working knowledge of asset information management
  • A team player and able to work autonomously
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills that would be described as accurate and friendly, but direct and persuasive
  • Highly organised with attention to detail
  • A willingness to travel - working locally, interstate, and internationally

If you are looking to challenge yourself and progress your career on a global stage, then apply now!

A career you'll love

A career with Datum360 is an opportunity to work with exciting technology, work with a great team and work with customers across the globe helping them with the data that they need for their assets. We love innovation, we are always learning, we work hard and we have fun!

How to apply
